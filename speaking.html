Freediving Through Fear: Mental Fitness to Unlock Capabilities We Don't Know We Have

At -79 metres on a single breath, Mental Fitness isn't optional — it's what keeps me alive. In this keynote, through storytelling and real-life experience, I teach your audience the 5 scientifically backed tools I rely on, and get them practising before they leave the room.
