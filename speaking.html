Signature Keynote

Freediving Through Fear: Deep Lessons for Life Above Water

At 100 metres below the surface, there is no room for panic, self-doubt, or distraction. In one of the world's most extreme environments, Helena learned that the mental tools needed to survive underwater are the same ones that help leaders and teams thrive above it.

In this keynote, Helena shares five practical mental fitness tools drawn from the world of competitive freediving — tools your audience can begin using immediately to manage pressure, prevent burnout, and perform at their best when the stakes are highest.

What Your Audience Will Learn

  • How to regulate your nervous system under extreme pressure
  • The breath-focus technique used by world-class athletes before high-stakes moments
  • Why embracing fear — rather than fighting it — is the key to peak performance
  • How to build mental resilience that prevents burnout before it starts
  • Creating a personal "mental fitness" routine for sustained high performance
