Signature Keynote

Freediving Through Fear: Mental Fitness to Unlock Capabilities We Don’t Know We Have

What would it mean for your organisation if your employees could consistently access clear thinking, sharp decision-making skills and confident leadership? They already have them, but chronic stress has locked these skills away behind decision paralysis, imposter syndrome and presenteeism, driving them towards burnout.

Some keynotes inspire for an hour but the lessons are forgotten by Tuesday. As a 4× World Champion freediver who rebuilt my life from the depths of severe depression, I teach Mental Fitness as the foundation of sustainable high performance. Not through the adrenaline required for fire walks or ice baths, but through tools that build Performance Architecture: the calm, focused clarity that gives us full access to our sharpest analytical and creative thinking.

Your audience won’t just hear about the tools. They’ll practise them, feel the benefits and be able to incorporate them immediately; sharpening decision-making, reducing burnout and reclaiming the performance these hidden costs have stolen.

Ready to help your audience discover what they’re truly capable of?
